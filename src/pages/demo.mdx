# Demo

This demo allows you to experience the features of our invoice collector without any risk. It is completely free but it has limitations because... well... it is a demo!

**Limitations:**
- Your collector will be disabled after the first collect.

**informations:**
- Your session lasts 10 minutes. After it expires, you have to request a new demo session.
- Your email is never stored, it acts as a unique identifier.
- Make sure to use the same email address next time.
- A verification code will be sent to your email along with the [Terms of Use](/terms-of-use).
